# NewsBites Editorial Planning Pack

This folder stores the durable planning material for the NewsBites editorial operation.

Files:
- `RESEARCH_FINDINGS_2026-04-06.md`
  - external research and source-backed conclusions
- `OPERATING_MODEL.md`
  - the recommended newsroom workflow, gates, and cost model
- `AGENT_SPECS.md`
  - role-by-role agent instructions and responsibilities
- `EDITORIAL_POLICY.md`
  - editorial standards, corrections, legal-risk guardrails, and AI-use policy
- `ARTICLE_DOSSIER_TEMPLATE.md`
  - the permanent on-disk evidence log template for each story

Intent:
- keep the public main page as a normal editorial news surface
- keep the app as the digestible short-form news surface
- optimize for quality, truthfulness, and low cost
- preserve evidence and decisions in local files, not chat history

Operating rule:
- do not treat these files as published policy automatically
- they are the working source of truth for configuring agents and editorial workflow
- if live behavior changes, update these files and log the change in `MASTER_PLAN.md`
